TimeMelt - Digital Detox Clock

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the TimeMelt - Digital Detox Clock Chrome extension ("Extension") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe and beneficial experience.

Information We Collect

The TimeMelt Extension requires access to certain browser permissions to function effectively. We collect the following types of information directly from your browser's local storage and active tabs:

  • Time Spent Data: We track the time you spend on websites that you have defined as "time-wasters" within the Extension. This data includes the domain name of the website and the duration of your activity on it. This information is processed and stored solely locally on your device within your browser's storage.
  • Monitored Website List: The list of websites you choose to monitor for time tracking is stored solely locally on your device within your browser's storage.
  • Active Tab Information: To accurately track time spent on websites, the Extension needs to access information about your currently active browser tabs (e.g., the URL of the active tab). This information is processed in real-time and used solely for the purpose of time tracking. We do not store your Browse history or transmit this information to external servers or any third parties.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected by the TimeMelt Extension is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To monitor and analyze the time you spend on defined "time-waster" websites.
  • To generate visual reports (e.g., pie charts) and lists summarizing your time spent on websites.
  • To enable you to customize the list of websites you wish to monitor.
  • To provide you with detailed breakdowns of your daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly activity.
  • To help you achieve your digital detox and time management goals.

Data Storage and Security

All data collected by the TimeMelt Extension (time spent data, monitored website list) is stored exclusively locally on your device within your Chrome browser's local storage. This means:

  • No Data is Sent Off Your Device: We do not transmit, share, or sell any of your personal usage data to our servers, external servers, or any third-party services. Your data remains entirely on your computer, under your control.
  • Data Security: The security of your data relies on the security of your browser and your device.
  • Data Persistence: Your data will persist locally within your browser's storage until you clear your browser data for the extension or uninstall the extension.

Third-Party Services

The TimeMelt Extension does not integrate with any third-party services that collect, store, or process your usage data. All functionalities operate locally within your browser.

Children's Privacy

Our Extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
